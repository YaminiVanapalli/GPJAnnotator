New Look…

The colors may have changed, but all the existing functionality, and more, is still in there.  Nose around and you’ll see all the new changes.

 

Graphics Handler Changes…

One of the changes in the ability to see thumbnails of the posted PDF files.  Before, you saw a generic icon for PDFs that required you to open them before seeing any hint of their content.  Now, they have a thumbnail, just like all the jpeg images.

 

Where did the Digital Image Library go?

For those of you who accessed the Digital Image Library from inside the Annotator’s interface, you may have noticed there is no longer a link to open it.  The Library, as you probably saw while entering the Annotator, has it’s own icon in the portal page, available after you log in.  Click that icon to enter the Library.  You will also find a link to the Digital Image Library in the Employee Portal, on the GPJ Tools tab.
